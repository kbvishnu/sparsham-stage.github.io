Facilitate their journey to school

Be part of our vision to reinforce their path to school.

Kerala Government provides free education in both Malayalam and English medium from lower primary to higher secondary. Apart from that schools provides free meals to the children.Encouraging a child to school will help to get a proper meal as well as education. We support all the needs like transportation, dress, books and other fees. In some exceptional cases we may support the family requirements after getting approval from EC.

Compassionate Interaction

A day programme to enagage and interact with lonliness people in our society.

There a many people around us are living a lonely life in their rooms during their last days or may be some kind of disablities. We often arrange some interactive sessions and have food with them. We tells stories, plays movies.

Awarness Campagins

Campagins to make people aware of things and stay out of fear.

We conduct sessions and seminars to spread awarness about various topics like blood donation, organ donation and more. Also our members are active in sharing valid information from trusted sources on the ocasion of any catastrophic disaster. We also encourage people to contribute directly to the official fund raising campigns conducted by the state and central governments.
